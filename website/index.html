About The Project

The system is useful for learning, demonstration, and basic crop health awareness, while showing how AI can support faster plant disease detection.

AI Technology Used

A trained AI model studies uploaded leaf images, compares them with learned disease patterns, and predicts the most likely class. Python and OpenCV support the image processing pipeline behind the prediction flow.

MobileNetV2 TensorFlow Keras Streamlit PlantVillage

Why It Matters

Early disease identification can help people respond sooner, understand crop health risks, and explore treatment guidance with more confidence. The interface is designed to stay simple while still giving quick results and practical recommendations.
